Lionel Messi

One of the best players of all-time

Here's a time line of Messi's life:

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina
  • 2000 - Messi travel to Barcelona's La Masia youth academy.
  • 2003 - Frank Rijkaard gives Messi his debut at age 16 in a friendly against Porto coached by Jose Mourinho
  • 2004 - Messi plays first official match for Barcelona at age 17
  • 2006 - Messi wins first Champions League title. During de 2005-06 season he has earned a spot in Barcelona's attack alongside Ronaldinho and S. Eto'o.
  • 2007 - Messi scores first hat trick for Barcelona in a 3 - 3 draw against Real Madrid.
  • 2009 - Messi wins his second Champions League after scoring to beat Manchester United in the final.
  • 2009 - Messi wins his firs Balon d'Or award for the best player in soccer.
  • 2011 - Messi wins his third Champions League. He score the winning goal over Manchester United in the final.
  • 2012 - Messi becomes Barcelona's all-time leading scorer with 672 goals for Barcelona.
  • 2014 - Messi becomes Spanish leagu's all-time leading scorer with 474 goals in La Liga.
  • 2015 - Messi wins his fourth Champions League after Barcelona beats Juventus in the final.
  • 2017 - Messi signs his las contract with Barcelona, a five-year deal lasting through June 30, 2021.
  • 2019 - Messi wins the Spanish League for a 10th and final time.
  • 2021 - Messi scores twice to lead Barcelona to a 4-0 win in the final of the Copa del Rey. Messi's seventh domestic cup takes his club record to 35 title for Barcelona.
  • 2021 - Messi signs 2 - year contact with Paris Saint-Germain. Now he is playing with Neymar his teamate in Bacelona.
